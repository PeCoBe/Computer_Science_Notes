Business Process Management

Business Process:

Collection of events, activities and decisions that involve a given number of actors and

resources.  Provide value to the business/clients

Examples

Order to cash

Procure to pay

Application to approval

Claim to settlement

Fault to resolution

Each process leads to a result:

Positive: give value to the business

Negative: take value away from the business
